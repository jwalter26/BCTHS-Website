Mission Statement:

The mission of the Berlin Charter Township Historical Society is to inspire an awareness and shared sense of community in our township heritage.


Our Vision:

The vision includes uniting collectively those people interested in the history of Berlin Charter Township and to maintain and promote it through the recording, gathering, preservation, edneation and display of the places, people, events and artifacts that are the cornerstone of our township.
