A membership in the Berlin Charter Township Historical Society is a great idea!

When you purchase or give a membership, you are also contributing an important gift to our community. With your membership, you help support the Berlin Charter Township Historical Society in its efforts to inspire an awareness and shared sense of community in our township heritage. In addition, you help ensure that the township’s past is kept alive, and that future residents of our community can experience the rich history that is unique to Berlin Township.

Member Benefits:

  • Advance notice of all Society-sponsored events, programs, lectures, etc.
  • Electronic copy of the Society newsletter (mailed version available upon request)
  • Discount to any fee-based event the Society hosts
  • Berlin Charter Township Historical Society membership card

Membership Levels:

Charter Member* – Available for the 2018-19 Membership Year Only!

  • Charter Annual Member – $30 first year, $15 annually thereafter
  • Charter Lifetime Member – $115 one-time only payment

Regular Member

  • Regular Annual Member – $15 annually
  • Regular Lifetime Member – $100 one-time only payment

*Unique Opportunity Available for the 2018-19 Membership Year Only!


In addition to providing the regular membership benefits described above, Charter Members receive:

  • High-quality, special edition Charter Member lapel pin (1” diameter, shown at left)
  • Charter Member certificate
  • Designation on the Charter Member “Honor Roll” for permanent display (location TBD)
  • Recognition in the first Society newsletter

Become a Charter Member and forever be a part of the legacy of Berlin Township!
