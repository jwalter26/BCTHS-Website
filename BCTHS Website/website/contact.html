About Us


The Berlin Charter Township Historical Society was formed in January 2018 by two local citizens inspired by the townshipâ€™s 150th anniversary to create an organization that would help preserve the rich history of Berlin Charter Township, Michigan.


The Society is a Michigan nonprofit corporation with 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status. A registered charity with the State of Michigan, it is governed by a Board of Directors selected from the general membership. The five founding Directors are:
